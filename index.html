About Our Class

Building BXA is a collaborative pop-up installation by BXA Intercollege Degree Programs students to create a welcoming, relaxing, and interactive lounge space. It is dedicated to interdisciplinary exploration at the intersection between artistic and academic scholarship. The Building BXA lounge, featuring custom-build modular furniture, samples of student work, and dynamic wall surfaces, will be open at the base of the CFA south stairwell from December 2-12. The class will officially launch the lounge during the School of Art's Open Studio Day on December 5, from 5-10pm, with a welcome and introduction by the builders at 7pm, and special programming to follow. The Building BXA lounge will also host special events throughout the duration of the installation – watch the BXA Intercollege Degree Programs Facebook page for updates and information.

The Space

Building BXA utilizes an underappreciated nook in the basement of CFA as the setting for our pop-up lounge. Generally ignored, this unused space has been transformed into a functional and enjoyable destination for all visitors to the building. The lounge makes this otherwise anonymous location a highly visible expression of the BXA community.
